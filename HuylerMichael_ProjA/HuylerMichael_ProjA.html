Scene Navigation
  • Press WASD to move camera
  • Press IJKL to rotate camera
  • Press Space to manipulate the particles
  • Press P to globally pause / unpause animation
  • Hold O for quick slo-mo
  • Press ↑↓ to change simulation speed
  • Press C to toggle trails
